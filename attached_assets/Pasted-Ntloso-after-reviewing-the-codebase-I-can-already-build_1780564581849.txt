SMC Pulse Predict (Liquidity Hunter)

User Guide

Purpose

SMC Pulse Predict is designed to identify the most likely next draw on liquidity using Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and ICT principles.

Instead of generating buy/sell signals, it answers:

“Where is price most likely being engineered to go next?”

The application does this by combining multiple analytical agents that evaluate:

* Market Structure
* Liquidity Pools
* Fair Value Gaps (FVGs)
* Order Blocks (OBs)
* Multi-Timeframe Confluence
* Institutional Intent

⸻

Main Workflow

Step 1: Select Market

At the top of the dashboard choose:

* Crypto
* Forex

Changing market type automatically updates the available symbols.

⸻

Step 2: Select Symbol

Examples:

Crypto:

* BTCUSDT
* ETHUSDT

Forex:

* EURUSD
* GBPUSD

The selected symbol becomes the focus of all agent analysis.

⸻

Step 3: Select Trading Style

The Timeframe selector has four modes:

Mode	Purpose
Scalp	Very short-term liquidity draws
Intraday	Same-day moves
Swing	Multi-day directional bias
All	Full market structure stack

⸻

Step 4: Review Multi-Timeframe Confluence

This is the first thing traders should examine.

The card displays:

* Bullish Draw
* Bearish Draw
* Mixed Conditions

Example:

▲ BULLISH DRAW
4↑ / 1↓

Meaning:

* 4 timeframes expect liquidity above
* 1 timeframe expects liquidity below

This indicates strong bullish alignment.

Tap the card to open the intelligence panel.

⸻

Timeframe Agent Cards

Each timeframe operates like its own analyst.

Examples:

* M5
* M15
* H1
* H4

Each card predicts:

Next Draw On Liquidity

Example:

▲ 108,500

Meaning:

The model believes liquidity above current price is the highest probability target.

⸻

Confidence Score

Example:

Conf 82%

Higher confidence means:

* Better structure alignment
* Stronger liquidity pools
* More confluence between agents

⸻

Alternative Draw

The system also provides:

Alt Target

This represents:

The second-most likely liquidity objective.

Understanding The Agents

The app uses several specialized agents.

⸻

Structure Agent

Tracks:

* MSS
* BOS
* Trend Bias

Outputs:

* Bullish
* Bearish
* Neutral

Questions answered:

* Who currently controls the market?
* Has structure shifted?

⸻

Liquidity Agent

Tracks:

BSL

Buy Side Liquidity

Typically:

* Equal highs
* Swing highs
* Obvious resistance

⸻

SSL

Sell Side Liquidity

Typically:

* Equal lows
* Swing lows
* Obvious support

Questions answered:

* Where are stops resting?
* Which liquidity pool is nearest?

⸻

Order Block Agent

Tracks:

* Bullish OBs
* Bearish OBs

Questions answered:

* Where did institutions last accumulate?
* Where may price retrace before continuation?

⸻

FVG Agent

Tracks:

* Bullish FVGs
* Bearish FVGs

Questions answered:

* Where is imbalance?
* Where might price seek rebalancing?

⸻

Timeframe Intelligence Panel

When you tap a timeframe card, the app opens the Intelligence Sheet.

This is the most powerful feature in the application.

The panel is divided into sections.

⸻

1. Structure

Shows:

* Current bias
* Last MSS
* MSS price level

Purpose:

Understand current market control.

⸻

2. Liquidity Map

Displays:

BSL Above

Potential upside liquidity targets.

SSL Below

Potential downside liquidity targets.

Equal Highs

Potential engineered target.

Equal Lows

Potential engineered target.

⸻

3. Imbalance Zones

Displays:

Bullish FVGs

Areas where price may retrace before continuing upward.

Bearish FVGs

Areas where price may retrace before continuing downward.

⸻

4. Order Flow

Displays:

* BOS
* MSS
* Displacement

Purpose:

Reveal whether institutions are aggressively repricing.

⸻

5. Confidence Drivers

Shows why the AI assigned its confidence score.

Examples:

* HTF alignment
* Nearby liquidity
* Strong displacement
* Structure agreement

This is your transparency layer.

⸻

Agent Pipeline (Live Reasoning)

One of the most unique features.

The application creates an internal conversation between agents.

You can watch:

* Structure Agent reasoning
* Liquidity Agent reasoning
* FVG Agent reasoning
* Confluence Agent reasoning

This exposes the logic behind the prediction.

⸻

Ask The Agents

At the bottom of the Intelligence Sheet is a chat box.

Example questions:

* Why is the draw bearish?
* Where is the strongest liquidity pool?
* Which FVG is most important?
* Is HTF aligned?
* What invalidates this setup?

The system then uses the current market context to answer.

Recommended Workflow (SMC Trader)

For someone using your trading methodology, I would recommend:

1. Select market and symbol.
2. Open Confluence panel.
3. Determine dominant liquidity draw.
4. Check HTF → LTF alignment.
5. Open highest-confidence timeframe.
6. Review liquidity map.
7. Review MSS/BOS.
8. Review FVGs and OBs.
9. Ask agents for confirmation.
10. Build trade plan manually.

The app should be treated as an institutional narrative engine, not a signal generator.
